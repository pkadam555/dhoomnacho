Delete Your Account

This page explains how to request deletion of your Dhoom Nacho (marketed globally as Doom Dance) account and associated data. The app is operated by Prasanna Kadam (Individual Developer).

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to dev.prasanna.kadam@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign in to the app.
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete my Dhoom Nacho account".
  3. We will confirm your request and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

If you signed in with Google, send the request from that Google account's email address so we can verify ownership.

What Is Deleted and What Is Kept

DataWhat happens
Account data — email address, name, sign-in credentials (Clerk)Deleted within 30 days of your request
Analytics profile and usage events — including monitored app names and usage minutes (PostHog)Deleted within 30 days of your request
Crash logs and diagnostics linked to your account (PostHog)Deleted within 30 days of your request
Data stored locally on your deviceRemoved when you uninstall the app
Advertising data (Google AdMob)Retained by Google per Google's privacy policy; not linked to your Dhoom Nacho account

There is no additional retention period beyond the 30-day processing window — once processed, deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete Your Data Without Deleting Your Account

You may also request deletion of some of your data while keeping your account — for example, your analytics history. Email dev.prasanna.kadam@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Dhoom Nacho data" and tell us which data you want removed. We will process the request within 30 days.

Questions

For anything else about your data, see our Privacy Policy or contact dev.prasanna.kadam@gmail.com.
