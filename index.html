How It Works

The Digital Bounty System

A simple 4-step loop that replaces passive dopamine scrolling with active physical movement.

1

You launch a social media app

Instagram, YouTube, TikTok — any app you've added to your personal "friction list" during setup.

2

Dhoom Nacho intercepts

Using Android Accessibility Services, the app detects the launch and overlays the Dance Break screen on top instantly.

3

You complete your dance break

A curated YouTube Shorts dance video plays for your chosen duration (1 – 5 min). You can't skip it — the player is locked.

4

You earn your access

Timer completes → access is granted for your daily allowance. Every session logs a completed "bounty" so you can track your movement over time.

Features

Built for real habit change

Every feature is designed around the core goal: make movement the natural path of least resistance.

🎬

Curated Dance Videos

YouTube Shorts — vertically optimised, auto-playing. A different dance video every session. Bollywood, K-pop, Hip-Hop and more.

🔒

Skip-Proof Player

JS-level touch interception prevents vertical swipe navigation. The video runs; there is no escape until the timer completes.

📱

Dynamic App Selection

The app reads your actual installed apps via Android accessibility APIs. Only add apps you genuinely want to friction-ise — no pre-set lists.

⏱️

Daily Usage Allowance

Set how many minutes per day of social media time you budget. The intervention triggers only after the allowance is exhausted.

🚨

Emergency Bypass

Tap a hidden corner 10 times for a 2-minute emergency unlock. Every bypass is logged locally so you can hold yourself accountable.

📶

Offline Resilience

No internet? The Manual Dance mode activates — a pulsing timer with motivational prompts. The habit loop holds even without connectivity.

🕛

Midnight Reset

Usage allowance resets automatically at midnight local time. Each day is a fresh start with no manual intervention required.

📊

Lightweight Analytics

Tracks bounties completed, interventions triggered, and session durations — to show you your progress over time without surveillance.

Privacy

Your phone, your data

We never read your messages, contacts, or content. Our accessibility permission is used only to detect which app is in the foreground.

🔑

Hashed Identifiers

All device IDs are salted and SHA-256 hashed locally before any analytics event is sent.

📵

No Content Scanning

Accessibility permission detects app package names only. Messages, photos, and contacts are never accessed.

💾

Local-First Storage

Usage data and bypass logs are stored on-device using Android SharedPreferences. Nothing is uploaded or sold.

🚫

No Ads Targeting

Any ads displayed are non-personalised by default. No behavioural profiling is built from your usage.
