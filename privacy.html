Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Dhoom Nacho (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. This policy applies to our mobile app and the docs website.

Data We Collect

We limit data collection to the minimum required to provide the app’s features:

  • Device identifiers: A salted SHA‑256 hash of a device identifier for lightweight analytics. The raw identifier is never uploaded.
  • Usage events: Non-personal events such as a completed dance session, app launches intercepted, session durations, and emergency bypass counts. These are tied to the hashed identifier only.
  • Email (optional): If you sign up for early access or email lists, we store your email to contact you. You can request deletion at any time (see Contact below).

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not read or upload the contents of your messages, photos, contacts, or other personal files.
  • We do not collect precise location, microphone recordings, camera images, or keystrokes.
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties.

Why We Request Accessibility Permission

The Android Accessibility permission is used only to detect which app is currently in the foreground so the app can intercept configured social apps and show the Dance Break overlay. We only read package names — we do not capture the content shown inside those apps.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Dhoom Nacho is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verified parental consent, we will delete that information promptly. If you believe we may have any such data, contact us (see below) and we will remove it.

How We Use Data

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the core experience (unlock rules, session tracking).
  • Generate anonymous analytics to understand engagement and reliability.
  • Send optional email updates to users who sign up for early access.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the data we hold, such as hashing identifiers before transmission and minimising stored fields. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data Deletion & Access

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, email us at dev.prasanna.kadam@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe and delete or anonymise data upon verified request.

Third Parties

We may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services receive only anonymised or hashed data necessary for their function. We do not share personal content with advertisers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will post the updated policy here with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, contact us at dev.prasanna.kadam@gmail.com.
